Welcome!

I am a Computational Scientist in Glaciology for the Climate Change Science Institute at Oak Ridge National Laboratory. I develop (primarily) python-based, open-source scientific software and am currently:

  • The technical and visionary lead of LIVVkit, a verification and validation toolkit for ice sheet models -- for both stand alone models and those coupled to Earth system models.
  • E3SM regular staff in the infrastructure group where I work on the case control system CIME, non-bit-for-bit climate reproducibility testing with EVV, and analysis of E3SM's coupled ice sheet model MALI.

See my research and software pages for more information.
